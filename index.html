Category Theory OctoberFest

Category Theory OctoberFest is an informal meeting, covering all areas of category theory and its applications. It is traditionally held in October.

Current Meeting

Octoberfest 2019 will be hosted at Johns Hopkins University, Baltimore, Maryland, over the last weekend of October, the 26th/27th. The meeting will start on the morning of Saturday the 26th and end the following afternoon. As is tradition, we are planning an introductory talk on Friday the 25th, joint with the Johns Hopkins Category Theory seminar.
