Category Theory OctoberFest 2018

Category Theory OctoberFest is an informal meeting, covering all areas of category theory and its applications. It is traditionally held in October.

Current Meeting

OctoberFest 2018 will take place in New York City at City College, which is part of the City University of New York, during the weekend of October 27/28, 2018.
